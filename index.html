Observabilidade, na teoria de controle, é uma medida quão bem podem os estados de um sistema ser inferidos a partir do conhecimento de suas saídas externas.

fonte: https://pt.wikipedia.org/wiki/Observabilidade

SLI - Service Level Indicator

  • Metrics over time
    • Request latency
    • Batch throughput
    • Failures per request
    • 99percentile or median
  • Ex: 95th percentile latency of homepage requests over past 5 minuest less than 300ms

SLO - Service Level Objective

  • The amount of time SLI could be out of specification
  • Ex: 95th percentile homepage SLI will succeed 99.9% over trailing year
  • Ex: lower than 1% rate of errors/month

SLA - Service Level Agreement

  • Business Agreement - define consequences of failing to deliver services
  • Associated with a SLO
  • Ex: Service credits if 95th percentile homepage SLI succeeds less than 99.5% over trailing year

Data Driven:

  • Observabilidade: Metrics, Structured Logs, Trace
  • Disponibilidade e Performance: Service Level Indicators(SLIs) / Service Level Objectives (SLOs)
  • Quality Gate: Coverage, Duplicated Lines, Maintainability Rating, Reliability Rating, Security Rating
